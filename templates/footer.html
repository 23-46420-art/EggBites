About Us

EggBites

Welcome to EggBites, your premier destination for freshly prepared, protein-rich egg creations. What started as a passion for nutritious, delicious food evolved into our purpose — to share wholesome flavors that bring energy and satisfaction to every meal. Each dish is crafted with attention to detail, using farm-fresh ingredients and culinary expertise — just like you'd prepare for your loved ones. With just a few clicks, you can enjoy chef-quality egg dishes delivered straight to your door.

Vision

EggBites aims to be everyone’s go-to online spot for delicious, freshly made egg bites that bring comfort and joy with every bite. We dream of making authentic flavors easy to enjoy anytime, anywhere — bringing café-quality food right to your doorstep with just a few taps.

Mission

Our mission is to make ordering egg bites simple, fast, and satisfying. We're committed to serving tasty, affordable, and freshly prepared dishes while giving customers a smooth and friendly online experience. At EggBites, we combine good food, great service, and a touch of home in every order.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

You can order your favorite egg dishes by browsing our menu, adding items to your cart, and checking out. Just choose your delivery address and payment method — it’s that easy!
We currently deliver within selected areas near our kitchen or partner locations. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your address during checkout.
Delivery time usually depends on your location and order volume. On average, you can expect your food to arrive within 30 to 40 minutes after confirmation.
We currently accept online payments through GCash, Maya, and PayPal for your convenience. More payment options may be added soon!
If your order is incorrect, delayed, or missing, please reach out to our Support Center or message us directly through our contact page. We’ll be happy to help and make it right.
